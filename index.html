CSU Hazardous Waste Storage Compliance Screening Tool

Built as a simplified engineering screening tool for hazardous waste storage conditions that might occur in university labs, maintenance shops, or campus support facilities. It helps identify obvious risk flags, documentation gaps, and time-based storage concerns using transparent rule-based logic.

Quickstart / User Guide

This tool is a simplified screening aid for hazardous waste storage conditions in campus facilities. Follow the steps below to use it correctly:

  • Step 1 – Enter Inputs: Provide generator category, waste type, storage duration, and observed conditions.
  • Step 2 – Review Tooltips: Hover over the ? icons to understand each input and expected values.
  • Step 3 – Run Assessment: Click "Run Assessment" to evaluate the storage conditions.
  • Step 4 – Interpret Results:
    • Compliant: No major or minor issues detected.
    • Warning: Minor concerns that should be reviewed.
    • Potential Violation: One or more serious issues requiring attention.
  • Step 5 – Take Action: Use the "Triggered Issues" and "Recommendations" sections to guide next steps.

Note: This is a screening tool and should not replace official regulatory review.
